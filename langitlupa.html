Langit Lupa

Larong Pinoy Traditional Game

How to Play Langit Lupa

Definition of Langit Lupa

Langit is the Filipino word for “heaven” and lupa is “earth”. The game’s name describes how the chaser cannot reach players how are elevated from the “earth”. Langit lupa is one of the best Philippine Traditional Games. Before, technology was not yet introduced to the children and so, having the creative mind of the young filipinos, they had created such a enjoyable game called Langit Lupa.

How to Play

In Langit-Lupa, one player is "it" and tries to tag the others who are running around on Earth. Players can run to Sky (a raised area) to be safe, but they can only stay there for a few seconds before returning to Earth. If a player is tagged on Earth, they are out or become the new tagger. The game continues until everyone has had a turn being "it."

Steps for Playing Langit Lupa

Step 1: Form the players it's up to you if you have 4 or more players, choose one ("Abal"), one on the ground ("Lupa") and the other player place on cement ("Langit"). .

Step 2: You must set it above in cement if the game begins since the "abal" will begin to catch the person who is in the ground.

Step 3: If the player is on the ground, the ("abal") will start trying to catch.

Step 4: When a player is positioned above ("langit"), the "abal" will wait for the player to go down.

Step 5: The only player who has been touched is already Abal, and Abal is already a player.
