Agawan Base

Larong Pinoy Traditional Game

How to Play Agawan Base

Definition of Agawan Base

Agawan Base is a traditional Filipino game in which two teams compete to capture the opponents base while defending their own base. The game involves strategy, speed, and teamwork.

How to Play

The game is typically played by two teams. Each team has a "base" that they must protect while attempting to capture the opposing teams base. To capture the base, a player must reach it without being tagged by the opposing team. Once a player touches the opposing base, they score a point for their team.

Steps for Playing Agawan Base

Step 1:Form Two Teams, Each team have a 3 player or more and have a designated base, which they must protect each base while attempting to capture the opposing team's base.

Step 2: Set Up the Bases Mark each team’s base (like a tree, line, or object). Both teams should know where their own base and the other team’s base are

Step 3: Capture the Base The goal is to run from your base to the other team’s base without getting tagged. If you touch their base, you score a point.

Step 4: Defend Your Base While trying to capture the other team’s base, protect your own. Tag anyone who gets too close to your base. If tagged, go back to your base before trying again.

Step 5: Win the Game The game ends when a team captures the other team’s base enough times or after a set time. You can also play until everyone has had a turn.
