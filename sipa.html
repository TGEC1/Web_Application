Takyan

Larong Pinoy Game Overview

How to Play Takyan

Definition of Takyan

Sipa is a traditional Filipino game where players use their feet to keep a small object, such as a coin or a rubber ball, in the air. It is similar to hacky sack but is played with more diverse techniques, including kicks, foot taps, and even head or knee touches.

How to Play

In Sipa, the objective is to keep the object off the ground by using your feet. The object is often a coin tied to a string or a small rubber ball. Players take turns attempting to keep it in the air, using any part of their body, but the main focus is on using the feet.

Steps for Playing Takyan

Step 1: It must be have a 4 players or more, and choose 1 for the thrower and the other players is to ("sipa").

Step 2: If the game begins the thrower will throw takyan, if the takyan will be drop the other player will help it.

Step 3: The player must continuously hit the takyan with his/her foot without dropping it on the ground. The player who did the most number of kicks without dropping the takyan will the game.
