Taguan

A Classic Filipino Game Overview

How to Play Taguan

Definition of Taguan

Taguan, also known as "Hide and Seek," is a Filipino children's game where one player is the seeker, and the others hide. The goal is for the seeker to find all the hidden players within a set time.

How to Play

One player counts while the others hide. Once the count is complete, the seeker attempts to find the hiders. The first person found becomes the new seeker for the next round.

Steps for Playing Taguan

Step 1: A player covers their hand over the others' pointing fingers. The player whose finger is chosen becomes the seeker.

Step 2: The seeker is start to counting and the players start to finding the spot that can hide.

Step 3: The seeker is getting closer to one of the hiders. Can the hider stay hidden?

Step 4: The seeker has found one of the hiders! The hider is safe, but the seeker is getting closer to the others.

Step 5: The hiders are trying to sneak past the seeker without getting caught. It's a race against time!
