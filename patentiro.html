Patentiro

A Classic Filipino Game Overview

How to Play Patentiro

Definition of Patentiro

Luksong Baka is a traditional Filipino game that involves players jumping over a person who is bent down like a "baka" (cow). As the game progresses, the height of the "baka" increases, making it harder for players to clear the jump.

How to Play

The game starts with one person crouching with their hands on their knees, forming the "baka" position. Other players take turns running and jumping over the "baka" without touching them. If the jumper clears the "baka," the "baka" player stands taller. The game continues in this way until a player fails to jump over the "baka" without touching them.

Steps for Playing Patentiro

Step 1: Choose the seeker and start counting while others hide.

Step 2: The seeker begins searching for hidden players after counting.

Step 3: If the seeker finds someone, that person must run to a safe zone before being tagged.

Step 4: If the seeker tags a player, they become the new seeker for the next round.

Step 5: The game continues with players taking turns as the seeker until everyone has had a chance.
